About Venture X

Venture X is a vibrant, student-led Entrepreneurship Club at SJCE, dedicated to turning dreams into reality through action, collaboration, and innovation. Supported by STEP (Science & Technology Entrepreneurs Park), the club empowers students to transform their ideas into structured goals, actionable plans, and impactful ventures.


Here, members engage in a variety of entrepreneurship-focused and growth-oriented activities such as startup mentorships, business idea development, leadership talks, and skill-building sessions that inspire innovation and leadership among students.
